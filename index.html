BristleBot

An IoT Thing

The IoT BristleBot is a WiFi enabled bristlebot that you can monitor and control over a Wifi connection. The bristlebot can be connected to the internet so that it can report is status and receive control information over an interent connection. As such it is an Internet of Things (IoT) device.

The IoT BristleBot has been developed to show how Engineers are able to take a fairly simple system, in this case a vibrating motor and tooth brush head, that only provides uncontrolled motion and by adding electronic sensors, control and communication, develop a system that can be driven where you or even drive itself autonomously.
